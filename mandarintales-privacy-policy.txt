Privacy Policy for Mandarin Tales
Last updated: 2025
Mandarin Tales ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and what choices you have regarding your data.
1. Information We Collect
Personal Information
Name (if provided during sign-up)
Email address
Profile photo (optional)
User ID
Usage & App Data
Story progress and reading activity
Preferences (character set, level selection, UI preferences)
Onboarding status
Session data (which screens or features are used)
Crash reports and performance metrics
Device Information
Device model and operating system version
Non-advertising device identifiers
App version
We do not collect precise GPS location or advertising identifiers.
2. How We Use Your Information
We use your information to:
Provide and operate the core app functionality
Create and manage your account
Sync your reading progress and preferences
Improve app performance and fix bugs
Understand how users interact with the app (analytics)
3. Third-Party Services
We use trusted third-party service providers to operate our app:
Firebase (Google)
Used for authentication, database storage, analytics, and crash reporting. Firebase may process user information as part of providing these services.
ElevenLabs
Used to generate audio narration within the app. Only the story text displayed in the app is processed to produce narration. Users do not upload custom text.
We do not share your data with advertisers, data brokers, or social media companies. All third-party services are used solely to provide app functionality and improve the user experience.
4. Data Retention
We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, associated personal data and app activity data will be removed from our systems within a reasonable timeframe. Some non-identifiable technical logs may be retained for security, troubleshooting, or legal compliance.
5. Your Rights
You may:
Access your personal information
Update or correct your information
Request deletion of your data
Delete your account at any time (when available)
6. Children’s Privacy
Mandarin Tales is not intended for children under 13 years of age, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected such information, we will delete it.
7. Security
We use industry-standard security measures, including encrypted connections and secure storage, to protect your data. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.
8. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page and within the app when appropriate. Your continued use of the app after changes are posted means you accept the updated policy.
